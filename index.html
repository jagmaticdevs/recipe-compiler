Adding This to Your Home Screen

Follow these steps and a "Recipe Box" icon will appear on your iPhone, just like any other app. You won't need to find this page again — just tap the icon.

⚠️ This only works in the Safari app. If you're using a different app to browse, open this page in Safari first.
1

Open the Recipe Compiler page in the Safari app on your iPhone.

2

Tap the Share button. It's the square with an arrow pointing up. It's usually at the bottom of the screen.

3

A list will pop up. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen."

4

Tap "Add" in the top right corner.

5

Done! Look on your Home Screen for the new icon — that's the Recipe Compiler. Tap it any time, just like any other app.
